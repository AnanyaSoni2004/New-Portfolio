About Me

Hi, I'm Ananya Soni — a driven and curious tech enthusiast currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence at Newton School of Technology, Rishihood University. I’m passionate about using technology to drive social impact, especially in education and accessibility.

From publishing a guide on Machine Learning to leading Booksyell — a platform providing CS education to over 1,500 underprivileged students — I love bringing ideas to life that make a difference. I’ve had the privilege of being a delegate at HPAIR (Harvard) and a student researcher at IIT Delhi, where I explored AR-based educational tools.

My toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Figma, and I enjoy solving DSA problems and exploring HCI, AI, and edtech. I thrive in collaborative environments, take initiative, and believe in continuous learning. Whether it's publishing, coding, or leading — I’m always up for building something meaningful.
